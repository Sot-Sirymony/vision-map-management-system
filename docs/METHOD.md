# Vision Mapping Method

The application uses a simple execution hierarchy:

```text
Vision Area -> Dream -> Goal -> Step -> Task
```

## Vision Area

A Vision Area is a major life or work category such as Career, Health, Finance, Family, Education, Business, or Leadership.

## Dream

A Dream is a meaningful future outcome inside one Vision Area. A good dream explains:
- what the user wants
- why it matters
- what success will look like
- when the user wants to reach it

## Goal

A Goal is a specific result needed to make a Dream real. Each Dream can have many Goals.

## Step

A Step is an ordered action stage under a Goal. Complex steps should be broken into Tasks.

## Task

A Task is a small executable action. Tasks include:
- owner
- due date
- priority
- status
- progress percent
- blocker reason when blocked

## Partners and Obstacles

Partners are mentors, experts, advisors, colleagues, resources, tools, or funders that help move work forward.

Obstacles record what is blocking progress. The app supports obstacle types such as knowledge, skill, time, money, motivation, partner, system, and decision.

## Reviews

Reviews help the user revise the plan. Supported review types:
- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Quarterly

Reviews should capture completed tasks, delayed tasks, blocked tasks, lessons, and next actions.
